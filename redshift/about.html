About Redshift

Welcome! RedShift is a place for me to post the things I learnt related to Physics, math & code. Redshift comes from the physics term redshift, which refers to an increase in wavelength i.e. light becoming 'redder'.

My main blog, neuristiq is extremely technical and dedicated to thermo-ML content, so I decided to create a new blog site. I'll still go deep of course, but I'll try to keep these posts short and readable for a more general audience.
